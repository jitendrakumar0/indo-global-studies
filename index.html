All Reviews
Viivi Vauhkonen
Finland
Hello, my name is Adelina and I want to thank the CIP agency in the city of Sumy for giving me the chance to study abroad! And I am very glad that I came across such professionals in my field! Everything was done quickly and qualitatively. There were many different colleges and I was really confused about the choice, but the CIP agency consulted on each of them and helped me quickly decide where it would be best for me to enroll. I didn't pass IELTS, so now I'm studying at the ICEAP language school. I like it very much at school, here I get exactly what I lacked in a normal school. I was very lucky with the teachers of this school, they are all cheerful, they behave with us like with friends (in moderation, of course), unlike Ukrainian teachers. Every day, coming to ICEAP, the teacher asks how we are doing, what's new, how everyone's day was and what plans we constantly play games, work in pairs, this helps a lot in learning English. They do everything so that lessons are not boring. Anyone who wants to know English perfectly or just learn it - choose the language school "ICEAP" in Toronto!!!
